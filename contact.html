SENIOR PRODUCT MARKETING MANAGER

We’re looking for a results-driven, senior product marketing expert to drive product positioning, product messaging, and go-to-market initiatives across our business. You’ll work closely with Product Management teams, along with other critical stakeholders including Sales, Engineering, and Support.

•  Results-driven individual who thrives in a highly dynamic environment.
•  A dynamic, motivated, resourceful, and results-oriented creative thinker and problem solver.

About

Fortem Technologies is a privately held, venture-backed company that delivers ultra small SWaP-C radar to enable, for the first time, completely autonomous beyond line of sight unmanned aircraft vehicle operations. The radar is also used on manned aircraft to provide the human pilot with assisted detect and avoid capability. Fully autonomous beyond line of sight operations are now possible due to this technology - now available for the first time - to commercial markets worldwide.
