IMSAR Announces Sale of Detect and Avoid Radar Technology to Fortem Technologies
Product Available from Fortem in July 2016

IMSAR LLC, leaders in miniaturized Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR), today announced the sale of their detect and avoid radar technology to Fortem Technologies. This technology powered IMSAR’s previously announced family of collision avoidance radar designed for the commercial Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS) market.

About

Fortem Technologies is a privately held, venture-backed company that delivers ultra small SWaP-C radar to enable, for the first time, completely autonomous beyond line of sight unmanned aircraft vehicle operations. The radar is also used on manned aircraft to provide the human pilot with assisted detect and avoid capability. Fully autonomous beyond line of sight operations are now possible due to this technology - now available for the first time - to commercial markets worldwide.
